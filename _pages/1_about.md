<br />

I got a Ph.D. in <a href="https://dcp.ufl.edu/urp/">Urban and Regional Planning</a> from the University of Florida, an M.S. in <a href="https://www.ues.pku.edu.cn/Home/Departments/Department_of_Urban_and_Regional_Planning/index.htm">Geography (Urban and Regional Planning)</a> from Peking University, and a B.Agric. in <a href="https://sola.bjfu.edu.cn/">Landscape Gardening</a> from BJFU.
<br />

My research centers on urban planning and spatial science, with broad interests in the built environment (infrastructure planning and facilities), as well as in hazard management, resilience, human mobility, and transportation.
<br />

My research methods include spatial analysis, big data analytics, spatial statistics, remote sensing, and data mining. I am proficient in GIS software, Python, and R. I also have training in 3D geospatial techniques integrated with VR, qualitative methods and community-based approaches, and urban and landscape design.
<br />

Beyond research, I have participated in planning practice and tool‐development projects in environmental and transportation fields, collaborating with government agencies in both the U.S. and China.
